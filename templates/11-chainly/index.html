Frequently asked questions.

Which chains do you support?+

Ethereum, major L2s and a growing list of EVM chains. New chains are added regularly.

Do you ever touch my keys?+

No. Chainly is non-custodial, you sign with your own wallet or multisig at all times.

Can I deploy to testnets for free?+

Yes. The Builder plan includes unlimited testnet deploys with no card required.

How does billing work?+

You pay a flat monthly fee per workspace, with usage-based add-ons. Cancel anytime.

Are contracts verified automatically?+

Paid plans verify source on the block explorer right after each successful deploy.
