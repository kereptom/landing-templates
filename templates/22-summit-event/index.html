Questions, answered.

When and where is Summit?+

It runs over two days at a single downtown venue. Exact dates and address are on the venue page.

Can I watch remotely?+

Yes. The free Community pass includes the main stage livestream and recordings afterward.

Are talks recorded?+

Every standard and pro ticket includes access to recordings of all sessions after the event.

Can I get a refund?+

Tickets are transferable up to one week before the event if your plans change.

Is there a student rate?+

Yes, discounted student passes are available with valid ID, subject to availability.

Ready to join us on stage-side?

Seats are limited and prices go up as we fill. Grab yours while early-bird lasts.
