Questions, answered.

I have never done yoga, is that ok?+

Absolutely. Many of our classes are made for first-timers, and our teachers guide you every step of the way.

Do I need to bring my own mat?+

No. We provide clean mats, blocks and props, though you are welcome to bring your own.

How do I book a class?+

Reserve online in seconds. Just pick a session, tap book, and we will save your spot.

What should I wear?+

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Bare feet on the mat, and a water bottle if you like.

Can I pause my membership?+

Yes. Monthly memberships can be paused anytime, no questions and no fees.
